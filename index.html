Navbar

Navbar 1

A navbar with all elements on the left.

Use a single child under div.navbar.

  • TITLE

  • Link 1
  • Link 2
  • Link 3
  • Link 4
					
<div class="navbar">
	<ul>
		<li><h1 class="title">TITLE</h1></li>
		<li><a href="#">Link 1</a></li>
		<li><a href="#">Link 2</a></li>
		<li><a href="#">Link 3</a></li>
		<li><a href="#">Link 4</a></li>
	</ul>
</div>

Navbar 2

A navbar with groups of elements on the left and the right.

Use two children under div.navbar.

  • TITLE

  • Mission
  • About
  • Contact

	<ul>
		<li><h1 class="title">TITLE</h1></li>
		<li><a href="#">Products</a></li>
		<li><a href="#">Mission</a></li>
	</ul>
	
	<ul>
		<li><a href="#">About</a></li>
		<li><a href="#">Contact</a></li>
	</ul>
</div>
